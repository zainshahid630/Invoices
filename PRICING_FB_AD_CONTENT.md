## Design Recommendations

### Visual Elements:
- Use a clean, modern gradient background (blue/purple or professional colors)
- Include pricing cards/boxes showing different plan tiers
- Add checkmark icons for features
- Use professional fonts (English: Poppins/Inter, Urdu: Noto Nastaliq Urdu)
- Include your logo
- Add a clear CTA button at the bottom

### Color Scheme:
- Primary: #3B82F6 (Blue)
- Secondary: #8B5CF6 (Purple)
- Accent: #10B981 (Green for checkmarks)
- Background: White or light gradient

### Dimensions:
- Facebook Feed: 1200 x 630px
- Instagram Square: 1080 x 1080px
- Instagram Story: 1080 x 1920px
