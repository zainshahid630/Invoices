## Posting Tips:

1. **Best Times to Post**: 
   - 9-11 AM (Morning office hours)
   - 1-3 PM (Lunch break)
   - 7-9 PM (Evening browsing)

2. **Content Mix**:
   - 40% Educational content
   - 30% Product features
   - 20% Customer success stories
   - 10% Promotional offers

3. **Visual Guidelines**:
   - Use your brand colors consistently
   - Include screenshots of actual dashboard
   - Show real results/metrics
   - Keep text minimal on images
   - Use high-quality graphics

4. **Call-to-Action**:
   - Always include "Link in bio"
   - Use action words: Start, Try, Get, Claim
   - Create urgency when appropriate

5. **Engagement**:
   - Respond to comments within 1 hour
   - Ask questions in captions
   - Use polls in Stories
   - Share user-generated content
